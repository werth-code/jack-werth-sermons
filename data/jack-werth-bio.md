# Pastor Jack Werth — biographical facts (for the memorial / About page)

Source: obituary, mcdonald-aeberli.com (McDonald-Aeberli Funeral Home).
Use these FACTS to write original, respectful prose — do not copy obituary text verbatim.

- **Full name:** Pastor Jack Arthur Werth
- **Born:** June 7, 1953, Pittsburgh, PA
- **Died:** September 15, 2023 (age 70)
- **Parents:** Arthur Jackson ("Jack") Werth and Dorothy Jean (Schomburger) Werth
- **Wife:** Rayna (Ervin) Werth — married 1975
- **Children:** Rebecca Cook, Sarah Spade, Jared Werth, Matthew Werth (the site's owner — Jack's son)
- **Grandchildren:** Makaylah, Ian, Christopher Cook; Levi Spade; Henry and Luke Werth
- **Siblings:** Karen Werth, Mark Werth, Carol Finley
- **Education:** Hampton High School; Arlington Baptist College (Arlington, TX), graduated 1975 as Valedictorian
- **Calling:** Called to preach at age 12
- **Early ministry:** Preached at Light of Life Mission and his home church
- **Pastorate:** Pastor of Liberty Baptist Church Reformed from 1984 until his death — ~39 years
- **Sermon archive:** www.lbref.org (the source of this library)
- **Years for a dedication line:** 1953–2023
