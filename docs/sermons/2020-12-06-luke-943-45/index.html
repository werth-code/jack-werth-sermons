Well, he’s a king. He’s the bridegroom. He’s the shepherd. But what do we find here in chapter two? He’s also the lily of the valley. He’s also the rose of Sharon. He’s unveiling more. And the more we fellowship with Christ, the more he tells us about. himself. Always expect more as you go to the scriptures. May God blessed to us this wonderful this morning.
