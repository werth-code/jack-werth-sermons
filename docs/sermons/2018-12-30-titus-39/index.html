My God bless to us his holy and sacred word this evening. Three men, John Newton, Lewis Weatherford, and Samuel Preston, arrived at the Ambassador Hotel in New York City. And as their cab left them off, the three men stared in amazement at the site of this 29-floor building. They checked in, took the penthouse suite at the very top of the building.
