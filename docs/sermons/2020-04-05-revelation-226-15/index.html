But there’s a thousand years to precede the loosed. There’s a long time, isn’t it? So in all three of these four, actually, these passages, we have the idea of it not Christ’s second coming, not being in hand, or a tearing or delaying, or a long time, doesn’t this tell us something? So when Revelation speaking, of what is at hand and Christ coming quickly, we must realize that Revelation cannot be speaking of the second coming of Christ’s personal, visible coming in these passages, but a judgment coming on apostate Israel.
