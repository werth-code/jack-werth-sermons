I want you to notice the humble attitude here of the minister. Paul is a great man. Paul has been elevated to the highest office in the church as an apostle. God did not make him the master of the church, the king of the church, the high priest of the church, or the head of the church. It was just a minister. What a contrast there is between this idea of minister and the vain and haughty titles that many in the church today take. Look at those of the Roman Catholic religion and their bishops and their pope. Those who claim to be the true successors and heirs of Paul and Peter certainly don’t resemble them in this. Friends, ministers are not to be lords over God’s heritage, but to be examples to the flock.
