You should be encouraged when your enemy is self-destruct, like a Hithafel committing suicide. You should be encouraged when you think upon God’s past deliverances of his people, Mahanayam. And then you should be encouraged when people are moved by God to help you. And out of nowhere, it seems, people come and help. And here we see that was shown by Maccare and Barzilli. Well, may God encourage us as his people to realize that he is the one who will take care of us. For our needs as well.
