He didn’t think much about it until someone who was a geologist happened to be traveling through the area, hiking, and he stopped by the cabin. And he noticed the doorstop, and he began by picking it up and closely examining it, and he discovered it was actually a large clump of coal, or excuse me, gold. In fact, it proved to be one of the largest gold nuggets ever found east of the Rockies. The man who owned the cabin simply didn’t realize what treasure that he had in that rock.
