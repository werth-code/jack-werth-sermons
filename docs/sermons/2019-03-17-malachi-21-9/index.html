With God’s help, I will make my life in California everything that he would have it be, and unlike you, will not fall. Sincerely, I won’t give the name. If you imagine receiving a letter like that, thousands of people have been in churches where there have been ministers, have been manipulative, have taken advantage of them, have led them astray, and they’ve been harmed by these ministers. God says he’s not going to let this happen forever. The time bomb’s going to go off eventually, and God’s judgment is going to fall. Let us close with word of prayer.
